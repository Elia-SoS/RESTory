Synopsis

No one knows where he comes from. They say he is a hero or a demon, a wizard or a madman. They say he spoke to gods and loved fairies. His name is Kvothe. Now he lives incognito, running a quiet inn in the middle of nowhere, but his past is about to catch up with him.

This is the story of how a young orphan became a legend. A story of survival, ambition, and the search for truth behind the myths. The Name of the Wind is a masterpiece of modern fantasy that will drag you into a world full of details and unforgettable characters.
